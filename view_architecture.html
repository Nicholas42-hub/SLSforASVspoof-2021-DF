üìã Architecture Details

  • SSL Features: XLS-R extracts features from all 24 transformer layers
  • Stage 1 (Temporal): Attention over time steps with temporal bias (emphasizes audio edges)
  • Stage 2 (Intra-Group): 8 groups √ó 3 layers with attention within each group + residual refinement
  • Stage 3 (Inter-Group): Hierarchical aggregation across 8 groups
  • Dual Training: Joint optimization with classification (CE) and contrastive learning (margin-based + supervised contrastive)
  • Semantic Levels: Acoustic (layers 1-8), Phonetic (layers 9-16), Linguistic (layers 17-24)
